About Me

A MEAL specialist who works
end-to-end, not just in one lane

Most M&E professionals can design a framework or analyse data or write a report. I do all three — from designing the data collection tool in KoBoToolbox to delivering a polished donor narrative that earns top marks.

With a Postgraduate Diploma in Monitoring & Evaluation from Uganda Management Institute and a Bachelor's in Statistics & Economics from Kyambogo University, I combine academic rigour with hard-won field experience across Uganda's health, SRHR, and agricultural development sectors.

I've supported programmes funded by UK Aid, USAID, UNICEF, and PEPFAR — working at every level from facility registers to donor dashboards. My UK Aid end-of-project report earned an official "A" performance rating — the highest possible.
